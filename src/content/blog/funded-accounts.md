### Funded Trading Accounts

With FundedNext, you don't have to wait to start earning — traders on the Stellar One Step and Two Step models get a 15% profit share during the challenge phase. Once funded, payouts can start as early as five business days for Stellar One Step accounts, while Stellar Lite accounts unlock bi-weekly withdrawals.

Drawdown limits vary by model, ranging from 3% to 5% daily and 6% to 10% overall, giving you some breathing room while keeping risk in check. Leverage is capped at 30:1 for the One Step, but jumps to 100:1 for Stellar Two Step and Lite, which is ideal if you prefer more flexibility.

Expert Advisors (EAs) are also permitted on MT4 and MT5 — great if you rely on automation or advanced strategies.

### Scaling Plan

FundedNext scales your account by 40% every four months if you:

- Grow the account by 10%
- Complete two successful withdrawals
- Finish the final cycle in profit

This continues up to a max of $4 million. So a $100K account could scale to $140K in 4 months, $196K in 8 months, and so on.

### How to Become a FundedNext Trader?

FundedNext offers three challenge models, each designed to suit a different type of trader.

**Stellar One Step** is ideal if you want to skip the long evaluation and get funded fast. It's a single-phase challenge with moderate risk limits and a 15% profit share from the moment you start.

**Stellar Two Step** is better suited for consistent traders who prefer a more structured path. The two-phase format gives you more flexibility on risk and strategy, while still offering a profit share during the challenge.

**Stellar Lite** is the most affordable option, designed for traders starting out or testing the waters. It has tighter drawdowns, no payouts during the challenge, and smaller account sizes — but it's a solid choice if you want lower upfront fees and still access a 95% split once funded.

You can customise your evaluation by adding the following (fees are percentage-based and applied to the base challenge fee):

- **Lifetime 95% Payout (+30%):** Lock in the full 95% profit split from day one, even before scaling.
- **Double Up (+40%):** Automatically double your funded account size upon successful challenge completion.
- **No Minimum Trading Days (+20%):** Remove the minimum trading day requirement.
- **10% Overall Loss Limit (+25%):** Increases the drawdown buffer for Stellar Lite accounts only.
- **Bi-Weekly Payouts (+15%):** Unlock faster payout schedules.
- **Swap-Free Account (+10%):** Removes overnight swap fees across all account types and sizes. Ideal for traders following religious or swing-trading strategies.

### Final Verdict

FundedNext ranks as the best funded account overall for traders wanting fast access to capital, strong earning potential, and flexible rules. With options for low-cost entry, fast withdrawals, and challenge-phase payouts, it's suited for serious traders focused on consistent growth.

## How We Choose the Best Funded Accounts

We evaluated funded accounts based on the trader experience after passing evaluation challenges. Our methodology prioritises factors like profit splits, trading rules, scaling opportunities, and ease of access to live capital. The goal was to identify firms offering fair and transparent funding models that support traders' long-term success. Here's what we focused on:

### 1. Profit Splits

Profit splits are a key metric for determining the earning potential of funded accounts. Firms with splits starting at 75% or higher, and opportunities to scale up to 90%-100%, scored higher.

### 2. Evaluation Challenges

We reviewed the structure and accessibility of evaluation challenges, including:

- **Profit Targets:** Reasonable targets, typically 8%-10% for Phase 1 and 4%-5% for Phase 2.
- **Drawdown Limits:** Flexible limits, such as 5% daily and 10% overall, that balance risk with opportunity.
- **Evaluation Process:** One-step, two-step, or instant funding options to cater to different trader preferences.

### 3. Scaling Plans
