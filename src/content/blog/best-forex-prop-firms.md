## What are forex prop trading firms?

Forex prop trading firms give forex traders the opportunity to trade with substantial capital provided by the firm rather than relying on their own funds. These firms evaluate traders through structured challenges or assessments to gauge their skill level, consistency, and risk management.

Traders who meet these requirements earn access to funded live accounts, allowing them to trade forex with a higher account balance than they might otherwise afford. Profits generated on these accounts are shared between the trader and the firm, making it possible for traders to earn from forex trading without risking more than the initial challenge fee.

## How do forex prop firms work?

Forex prop trading firms offer traders a pathway to access greater capital for forex markets, beginning with an evaluation phase. In this phase, you partake in a challenge where you are aiming to meet specific performance targets and risk management requirements, such as a 10% profit target with daily and overall drawdown limits of 5% and 10%. Success in this evaluation phase demonstrates your skills and risk management ability, qualifying you for a funded account backed by the prop firm's capital.

Once you reach the funded trader stage, you receive a profit share, typically between 80-90%, while the firm retains a smaller share. To encourage ongoing success, prop trading firms often conduct regular assessments and may offer scaling plans, allowing you to grow your capital as you demonstrate consistent, profitable trading performance.

This model provides a structured, low-risk way for forex traders to access significant capital without risking personal funds, aside from any initial evaluation fees.
